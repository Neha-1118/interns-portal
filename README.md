# Recruitment & Talent Management Portal

## Overview

The Recruitment & Talent Management Portal is a centralized platform designed to simplify the recruitment, assessment, and onboarding process for engineering professionals and interns.

Applicants from various engineering disciplines, including Chemical, Mechanical, Electrical, Instrumentation, Civil, IT, and other domains, can register through the portal, submit their profiles, participate in assessments, and progress through the organization's recruitment workflow.

The portal is being developed in multiple phases, with each phase introducing new capabilities to support a scalable and efficient hiring ecosystem.

> **Status:** 🚧 Prototype (Phase 1)

---

## Current Features

- Applicant Registration
- Profile Management
- Resume (CV) Upload
- Technical Domain Selection
- Skills & Experience Details
- Online Assessment Support
- Project Assignment (Planned)
- Team Collaboration (Planned)
- Applicant Progress Tracking

---

## Planned Features

- ATS Resume Screening
- Online Technical Tests
- Interview Scheduling
- Live Project Allocation
- Internship Management
- Performance Analytics
- HR & Admin Dashboard
- Email & Notification System

- Reporting & Analytics

## Project Vision

To build a unified platform that enables organizations to efficiently identify, assess, recruit, and manage engineering talent through a modern, scalable, and user-friendly digital experience.
